About The Murk
Built in Portland.
Built in the dark.

The Murk Audio LLC is an independent audio drama production studio and podcast network founded in Portland, Oregon. We make original audio fiction — scripted, produced, and distributed without a label or a network. Veteran-owned. Independently operated. Just the work.
